Dakar

Fun facts about Dakar!

Lake Retba, also known as Lac Rose, is one of Senegal’s most mesmerizing natural wonders, famous for its striking pink-hued waters caused by high salt content and the presence of Dunaliella salina algae. Situated northeast of Dakar, this saltwater lake is surrounded by vibrant sand dunes and serves as a hub of local activity. Salt harvesting is a major livelihood here, and visitors can witness workers collecting and piling glistening salt mounds along the shore. The area also offers opportunities for adventure, such as quad biking on the dunes or floating effortlessly in the lake’s buoyant waters. Nearby, local communities celebrate Senegalese culture with traditional music, dance, and mouthwatering cuisine. Enjoying dishes like thiéboudienne (spiced fish and rice) or grilled fish fresh from the Atlantic adds to the experience. Lake Retba, with its stunning beauty and cultural charm, is a destination that captures the spirit of Senegal.
